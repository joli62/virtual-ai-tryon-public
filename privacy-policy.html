Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 16, 2025

Virtual AI Try-On ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Key Points:
  • We process images only to generate virtual try-on results
  • Your photos are stored securely and are not shared publicly
  • You can use the app as a guest without creating an account
  • We use industry-standard encryption for payment processing

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address and password (if you create an account)
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Stripe (we do not store credit card numbers)
  • Images: Photos you upload for virtual try-on generation
  • OAuth Data: If you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your email and basic profile information

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Features used, generation history, app interactions
  • Technical Data: IP address, app version, crash reports

1.3 Guest Mode

When using the app without an account, we collect:

  • Device identifier (to track free tier usage)
  • Ad watch data (for free credits)
  • Generated images (stored locally on your device)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide Services: Generate AI try-on images using your uploaded photos
  • Process Payments: Handle subscription and credit pack purchases
  • Account Management: Authenticate users and sync data across devices
  • Free Tier Management: Track daily usage limits and ad watches
  • Improve Services: Analyze usage patterns and fix bugs
  • Customer Support: Respond to inquiries and resolve issues
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws and regulations

3. Image Processing & AI

What Happens to Your Photos:

  • Images are uploaded to our secure servers (AWS S3)
  • AI processing is performed by Replicate API
  • Generated results are stored in your gallery
  • Images are encrypted during transmission and storage
  • We do NOT: Use your images for training AI models, share them publicly, or sell them to third parties

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

4.1 AI Processing

  • Replicate: Processes images to generate try-on results
  • Privacy Policy: replicate.com/privacy

4.2 Payment Processing

  • Stripe: Handles all payment transactions
  • Privacy Policy: stripe.com/privacy

4.3 Authentication

  • Google OAuth: Optional sign-in with Google
  • Apple Sign-In: Optional sign-in with Apple (iOS only)

4.4 Advertising

  • Google AdMob: Serves ads for free tier users
  • Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

4.5 Cloud Services

  • AWS S3: Secure image storage
  • Neon: Database hosting

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
  • Secure Storage: Images stored in AWS S3 with private access controls
  • Password Security: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt
  • Payment Security: PCI-compliant payment processing via Stripe
  • Access Control: Limited employee access to user data

Data Retention:

  • Generated images: Retained until you delete them
  • Account data: Retained while your account is active
  • Payment records: Retained for 7 years (tax compliance)

6. Your Rights & Choices

6.1 Access & Control

  • View Your Data: Access your account information and generation history in the app
  • Delete Images: Remove individual generated images from your gallery
  • Export Data: Request a copy of your data by contacting support
  • Delete Account: Request account deletion (contact support)

6.2 Marketing Communications

  • We do not currently send marketing emails
  • If we introduce marketing communications, you can opt out at any time

6.3 Ad Preferences

  • Free tier users will see ads
  • Upgrade to a paid plan to remove ads
  • Adjust ad personalization in your device settings

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13 (or 16 in Europe). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn we have collected information from a child, we will delete it immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure adequate safeguards are in place for such transfers, including:

  • Standard contractual clauses
